Potato Thattikkoottu

Introduction:

Potato Thattikkoottu is a delicious South Indian dish that combines crispy potatoes with fragrant spices. It's an easy and flavorful side that pairs well with rice or can be enjoyed as a snack.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 medium potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 1-2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh curry leaves
  • Chopped coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin slices.
  2. Boil in salted water until tender. Drain.
  3. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add cumin seeds, red chilies, green chilies, and onions. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
  5. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Stir for a minute.
  6. Add boiled potatoes and toss gently to coat with spices. Fry until golden and crispy.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Enjoy this flavorful dish and make it a regular feature in your meals!
