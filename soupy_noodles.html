Knorr Soupy Noodles Recipe

Introduction

Knorr Soupy Noodles are a quick and tasty meal option perfect for busy days or when you're craving something warm and comforting. This instant noodle dish combines savory flavors with a delightful broth, making it a popular choice among both kids and adults. With just a few additional ingredients, you can elevate your soupy noodles to a whole new level, adding nutrients and taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet of Knorr Soupy Noodles (any flavor of your choice)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (like peas, carrots, and corn) â€“ fresh or frozen
  • 1 boiled egg (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce (optional, for added flavor)
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Chili flakes or pepper (optional, for spice)

Instructions

  1. In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil over medium heat.
  2. Add the mixed vegetables to the pot and cook for about 2-3 minutes until they are tender.
  3. Break the Knorr Soupy Noodles into halves and add them to the boiling water.
  4. Stir in the seasoning sachet that comes with the noodles and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  5. If adding soy sauce, chili flakes, or boiled egg, incorporate them now and stir well.
  6. Remove the pot from heat and pour the soupy noodles into a bowl. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Your delicious Knorr Soupy Noodles are ready to be enjoyed!

Tips

Feel free to customize your noodles by adding proteins like chicken, shrimp, or tofu. Experiment with different vegetables based on your preference!
